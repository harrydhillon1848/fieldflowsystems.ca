Operational workflow systems for high-risk, procedure-driven environments

FieldFlow Systems builds digital workflow and safety systems for operations where permits, procedures, and approvals are not paperwork — they are controls.

We work in environments such as marine terminals, barging operations, ports, and logistics facilities where work is performed under standing orders, regulated permits, and real operational risk.

Our systems digitize and structure:

  • Operational permits (e.g. confined space, hot work)
  • SOPs and standing orders
  • Role-based approvals and sign-offs
  • Incident and safety reporting

We do not sell off-the-shelf software. We build operator-informed systems, prototype them in the field, and refine them through controlled pilots before any broader implementation.

If you are responsible for operations, safety, or compliance and want to explore whether a focused digital workflow system makes sense for your environment, we start with a conversation.
